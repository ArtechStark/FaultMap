My research projects relates to the prioritization of base layer control loops from a maintenance perspective on a plant-wide scale.
The fields of fault detection, fault root cause analysis and process supervision is closely related to this project.
I plan to make use of a signed directed graph (SDG) of the plant combined with meta-data concerning the performance of individual loops and the economical value of the streams.
A node-ranking algorithm related to the PageRank algorithm of Google will be adapted to the special considerations involved in chemical plant networks.
